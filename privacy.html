Last Updated: October 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MealMind ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you register (if applicable), we may collect your email address, username, and profile information.
  • User Content: Recipe preferences, dietary restrictions, saved recipes, and meal logs you create within the App.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction times, crash reports, and performance diagnostics.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location data unless you explicitly grant permission for location-based features.

2.3 Third-Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Cloud Firestore (if used), Analytics, and Crashlytics.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): For serving advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data.
  • AI Model Providers: We use external AI APIs (e.g., Zhipu AI GLM) to generate recipe content. User input (ingredients, preferences) is sent to these services but is not stored by us beyond the session.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App's features (recipe generation, meal logging, nutrition tracking).
  • Personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • Communicate with you (e.g., updates, support responses).
  • Display advertisements (via AdMob) and measure ad performance.
  • Analyze usage patterns and fix bugs using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google), AdMob, AI model providers, and analytics services under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the App's services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your profile information within the App.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account by emailing us at wuannan1998@gmail.com.
  • Opt-Out of Ads: You can limit ad tracking via your device settings (iOS: "Limit Ad Tracking"; Android: "Opt out of Ads Personalization").
  • Withdraw Consent: If data processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (this may limit App functionality).

7. Children's Privacy

MealMind is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
